Discover more in our FAQs

What do I need to start learning Japanese with ProLingo?

Just your curiosity and eagerness to explore! There's no need for prior knowledge or materials. We'll guide you from the very beginning, whether you're starting from scratch or have some basic understanding.

Can I learn Japanese at my own pace?

Absolutely! Whether you're a speed-learner who wants to soak up as much as possible in a short time, or someone who prefers a more leisurely pace, our platform is flexible to your needs.

Are there any opportunities to practice speaking Japanese?

Yes! We offer language exchange sessions where you can chat with fellow learners and native speakers. You'll also have speaking exercises within our lessons, where you can record yourself and get feedback on your pronunciation and fluency

How will I know if I'm making progress in my Japanese studies?

Throughout your learning journey, you'll have regular assessments and quizzes to test your knowledge. You'll also notice practical improvements, like being able to understand more Japanese in movies, or when you have conversations with native speakers.

What if I need extra help or have questions during the course?

We're here for you every step of the way! Our support team is just a click or a call away. Whether you're stuck on a grammar point, need clarification on a vocabulary word, or have general questions about the course, we'll respond promptly.
